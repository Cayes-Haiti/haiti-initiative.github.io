Projects

Conferences

The purpose of the conference is to educate the public about the progression and the impact of climate change in Haiti. The larger goal of the conference and future conferences, is bring together community activists, politicians, and agronomists to collaborate in preparation for the effects of climate change locally.

Donation Drive

At the request of the grassroots organizers we are working with the rural towns in the southern region of Haiti, we collecting barrels of donations to be stored in the town halls ahead of hurricane season. Please drop off all donations to the Democracy Center (45 Mt. Auburn St. Cambridge, MA). Please review the list of requested items. Support Haitians in Haiti as they prepare for a expectantly brutal hurricane season.

The Plastic Project

The Plastic Project is a museum to teach people about the impacts of plastics on the environment. We hope to later include a plastic recycling plant on the same property. This project is in its infancy. So if you know any plastic engineers, please have them contact us.
